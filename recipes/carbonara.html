Pasta alla Carbonara

Ingredienti

  • Pasta 100g
  • Guanciale 50g
  • Egg Yolks 3
  • Pecorino Romano 30g
  • Salt q.s.
  • Black Pepper q.s.

Procedimento

  1. Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil
  2. When the water is boiling add a teaspoon of salt, swirl it to melt it and then add the pasta. Make sure that the pasta doesn't stick together or to the bottom of the pan stirring it every now and then
  3. While water is heating up mix the Pecorino Romano, Eggo Yolks and Black Pepper in a bowl, it should result in a thick paste
  4. In a pan, start cooking the Guanciale, cut in stripes or cubes, until crispy and the fat is well rendered
  5. When the pasta is cooked, strain it and put it int he same pan of the Guanciale
  6. Turn the heat off, and add the Egg Yolk and Pecorino Romano mix to the pasta, stirring it constantly to cook the eggs with the residual heat of the pan. Make sure to move it constantly in order to not overcook the eggs touching the hot pan
  7. Serve as a nest in a plate, you can top it with more Pecorino Romano and Black Pepper
